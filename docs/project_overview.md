# Project Overview

## Background

Space debris is one of the fastest growing risks in modern space operations. Thousands of inactive satellites, rocket fragments, and collision debris orbit Earth.

Even small debris can damage satellites traveling at extremely high velocities.

Organizations such as NASA, ESA, and ISRO monitor debris to prevent catastrophic satellite collisions.

## Problem Statement

Develop an artificial intelligence system capable of detecting space debris from satellite imagery and predicting potential collision events.

## Objectives

1. Detect debris objects in satellite imagery using deep learning.
2. Track debris trajectories using orbital prediction models.
3. Estimate collision risks between debris and active satellites.
4. Visualize orbital movements using an interactive dashboard.

## Expected Outcome

A working AI platform capable of assisting space situational awareness through automated debris detection and risk analysis.
